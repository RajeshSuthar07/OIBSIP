Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

1931 – 2015

“Dream is not that which you see while sleeping, it is something that does not let you sleep.”

Biography

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was a great Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He was known as the “Missile Man of India” for his contribution to India’s missile and space programs. He inspired millions of students to dream big and work hard through his simple life and powerful words.
